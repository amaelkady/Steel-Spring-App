# The Steel Spring App

An app for estimating the full-range force-defromation response of different steel members and connections based on the most recent analytical and empirical models.

The app (v1.2511) includes models for:
- Steel I-shaped beam-columns
- Steel I-shpaed beams as part of rigid welded, bolted, and RBS connections
- Steel web panel zone in I-shpaed sections
- Partial-strength extended endplate connections
- Flush endplate connections
